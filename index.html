Our Services

Library

Library is computerized at functional label.The library is spread over an area of 800 Sq. meters. To give an environment for study library has 3 reading hall with total seating capacity of 200 students. It has a collection of more than 92337 books of Indian and foreign editions with approx. 7859 titles.

Computer Lab

There are more than 35 spacious computer labs equipped with about 1250 computer sets with the latest configurations and the state-of -the-art software. All the computer systems are connected by LAN & 24 hour's Internet connectivity is provided for the benefit of students as well as faculty members.

Lecture Hall

Lecture Hall and rooms are architecturally well designed and are well ventilated. The furniture in the rooms is economically designed for comfort sitting of the students. In addition to the traditional blackboards, to make the teaching-learning process multidimensional ,LCD projectors are used.

Safe Transportation

The institute has its own fleet of buses which not only take care of daily transportation , but are also used for students outings like NSS programmed, entertainment and study tours, industrial visits etc.

Cafeteria

The cafeteria has a modern kitchen with the latest appliances to provide for Indian and Continental cuisine which includes snacks, fruit juice, tea, milk, coffee, working meals, sandwich, pizza, burgers, noodles, curd, lassi etc. The interior is tastefully crafted to provide the much needed relief and soothing environment.

Hostel

Our Big hostel provides2 seater Room with attached bathroom and other ancillary facilities. The management of the institute is very particular that the rooms are kept clear, maintain hygienic surroundings. Internet facility is also available in both hostels boys and girls.Both hostels are completely WiFi.
